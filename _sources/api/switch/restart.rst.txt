Restart Switch
==============

This platform allows you to restart your ESP8266/ESP32 with a simple MQTT message.

Example Usage
-------------

.. code-block:: cpp

    App.make_restart_switch("Livingroom Restart");

.. cpp:namespace:: nullptr

See :cpp:func:`Application::make_restart_switch`.
